### Example: Animal Type

Write a program that prints the type of the animal depending on its name: 

* dog -> **mammal**
* crocodile, tortoise, snake -> **reptile**
* others -> **unknown**

|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
|tortoise|reptile|dog|mammal|elephant|unknown|
