# Problem: \* Draw a Spiral Triangle with the Turtle

Add a \[**Triangle**\] button that draws three triangles with 22 beams each, as on the figure below:

![](/assets/chapter-5-images/13.Turtle-graphics-17.png)

**Hint:**
